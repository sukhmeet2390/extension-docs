How it works

1

Open Teams in Chrome

Go to teams.cloud.microsoft. The floating Privacy Blur widget appears automatically so you can prep before a client call, demo, or meeting.

2

Pick the zones you want hidden

Toggle messages, sender names, chat list, avatars, previews, attachments, compose box, and notification badges before you share your screen.

3

Hover to reveal privately

Need to check a message yourself? Hover over blurred content locally while your audience still sees the blur during the live share.

Frequently asked questions

How much does Privacy Blur for Teams cost?
You can install it free and start blurring right away. Pro is $3/month or $29/year for persistent settings, keyword rules, sender rules, and shortcuts. Cancel anytime.
Does it send Teams messages to a server?
No Teams message content, usernames, avatars, or files are sent to Amrita Labs. Blur logic runs locally in your browser. Anonymous product analytics and billing events are covered in the privacy policy.
Who is this built for?
Corporate users, consultants, customer-facing teams, managers, and anyone who presents Microsoft Teams during client calls, sales demos, board reviews, or all-hands meetings.
Does it work with the Teams desktop app?
Privacy Blur works on the Teams web app at teams.cloud.microsoft in Chrome. The desktop app does not support Chrome extensions.
