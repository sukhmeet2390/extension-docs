Privacy Policy — Privacy Blur for Teams

Effective Date: 2026-07-23 · Last Updated: 2026-07-23

Summary

Privacy Blur for Teams does not collect, store, or transmit any of your Microsoft Teams messages, contacts, files, or personal data. The extension runs entirely in your browser on teams.cloud.microsoft. No Teams content leaves your device.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Message content, usernames, or channel names
  • Files, images, videos, or media shared in Teams
  • Your Microsoft credentials or account details
  • Browsing history or activity outside of teams.cloud.microsoft
  • Any personally identifiable information (PII)

Data We Store Locally

The following is stored in chrome.storage.sync (synced across your Chrome browsers, never sent to our servers):

  • Your blur zone preferences (which zones are enabled or disabled)
  • Blur intensity settings
  • Panel position (stored in localStorage)
  • Trial start timestamp

Analytics

We collect minimal, anonymous usage events (for example, "extension installed" and "zone toggled") via PostHog to understand which features are used. These events contain:

  • A random anonymous ID (not linked to your identity)
  • Event name and basic properties (for example, which zone was toggled)
  • Extension version

We do not collect any Teams content, URLs, usernames, or anything that could identify you or your contacts.

Payments

Premium features are processed by ExtensionPay (powered by Stripe). We never see or store your payment card details. Stripe handles payment data under its own privacy policy.

Permissions

  • storage: Save your blur preferences across sessions
  • host_permissions (teams.cloud.microsoft): Inject the blur overlay on Microsoft Teams only

Third-Party Services

  • PostHog (analytics) — Privacy Policy
  • ExtensionPay / Stripe (payments) — Privacy Policy

Retention

Local preferences are stored until you uninstall the extension or clear browser data. Anonymous analytics events are retained for 12 months and then deleted.

Rights

You can delete all local data by uninstalling the extension. To request deletion of analytics data, contact us at hello@amrita-labs.com.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted at this URL. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
