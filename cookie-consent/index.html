Frequently asked questions

Is Amrita Cookie Consent free?
Yes. The core cookie consent banner is free. Handles GDPR, CCPA, and Google Consent Mode v2 out of the box.
Does it support Google Consent Mode v2?
Yes. Automatically sends consent signals to Google Analytics 4 and Google Ads. No manual tag configuration needed.
Does it auto-detect visitor location?
Yes. Detects EU/EEA visitors and shows the full consent banner. Non-EU visitors see a simplified notice or no banner, depending on your settings.
