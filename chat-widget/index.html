No. Messages from visitors go directly to your email. No dashboard to log into, no separate account to manage.
Is it free?
Yes. The core chat widget is free. Install from the Shopify App Store.
Will it slow down my store?
No. The widget is under 15KB and loads asynchronously. Zero impact on page speed.
