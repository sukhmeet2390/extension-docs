Frequently asked questions

Is EmailSubjectScore Pro free?
Yes. 50 AI scores per month, no signup, no credit card. Structural scoring (spam trigger detection) is always unlimited.
What data does it collect?
Only anonymous device ID for rate limiting. Subject line text is sent to Azure OpenAI for scoring but processed ephemerally — never stored. Image and page content never leave your browser.
Does it work with Google Workspace?
Yes. Works with personal Gmail and Google Workspace accounts. The extension activates in any Gmail compose window.
Does it auto-modify my emails?
Never. AI rewrite suggestions are copy-to-clipboard only. You paste what you want. The extension never changes your email content.
How is it different from SendCheckIt or CoSchedule?
EmailSubjectScore Pro scores inside Gmail as you type — no tab-switching or copy-pasting into external tools. It also provides AI rewrite suggestions, not just a score.
