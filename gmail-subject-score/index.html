Questions

Is it really free?

Yes — 50 scores every month at no cost, no signup and no credit card. Unlimited scoring is a paid upgrade.

Can it read my emails?

No. The extension only sends the subject line you're writing to score it. It never reads your inbox or email bodies.

Which email does it work with?

Gmail, right inside the compose window. More providers may come later.

How are the rewrites generated?

An AI model rewrites your subject for clarity and reply-worthiness, then re-scores the result so you can see the improvement.
