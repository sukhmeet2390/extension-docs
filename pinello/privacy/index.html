Privacy Policy — Pinello

Effective Date: 2026-06-15 · Last Updated: 2026-07-15

1. Who We Are

Pinello is operated by Amrita Labs, based in Canada.
Contact: hello@amrita-labs.com

2. What This Extension Does

Pinello scores Pinterest pins across 5 dimensions — image quality, board fit, title SEO, description optimization, and hashtag usage — in real time as you create pins. It also offers AI-powered rewrite suggestions for text fields.

3. Data We Collect

We collect only the following:

We do NOT collect:

  • Your Pinterest account credentials or login tokens
  • Pin images (analyzed 100% locally in your browser)
  • Browsing history or pages visited beyond Pinterest
  • Any personally identifiable information without explicit account creation

4. What Leaves Your Browser

When you trigger an AI score or rewrite, the extension sends:

  • Anonymous device ID (no name, no email)
  • Pin title and description text (ephemeral — processed and immediately discarded, never stored)
  • Diagnostic metadata (extension version, browser version)

Image analysis is 100% local — pin images never leave your browser.

All transmission is encrypted via HTTPS / TLS 1.3.

Important: Pin text is sent to Azure OpenAI (Microsoft) for AI scoring. The text is processed ephemerally and is not stored by us or used for model training. See Microsoft's data privacy commitments.

8. Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for users under 13 (COPPA) or under 16 in the EU (GDPR). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Security

This extension uses AI (GPT-4.1-mini via Azure OpenAI) to score pin text and generate alternatives. All AI-generated content is clearly labeled in the interface.
