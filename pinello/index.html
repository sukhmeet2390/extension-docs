Frequently asked questions

Is Pinello free?
Yes. 50 AI scores per month, no signup, no credit card. Structural scoring (image analysis, hashtag counting, keyword overlap) is always unlimited.
What data does Pinello collect?
Only anonymous device ID for rate limiting. Title/description text sent to Azure OpenAI for AI features is processed ephemerally — never stored. Image analysis is 100% local in your browser.
Does Pinello modify my pins?
Never. AI rewrite suggestions are copy-to-clipboard only. You paste what you want. Pinello never auto-edits your pin content.
Does it work on all Pinterest domains?
Yes. Works on pinterest.com and all country variants (ca.pinterest.com, uk.pinterest.com, etc.) for personal, business, and creator accounts.
How is Pinello different from Tailwind?
Tailwind is a scheduling tool. Pinello scores your pin quality BEFORE you publish — image, board fit, title, description, and hashtags rated in real time inside the pin-creation modal.
