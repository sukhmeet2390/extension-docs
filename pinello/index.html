How it works

1

Open Pinterest's Create Pin

Navigate to the pin-creation tool on Pinterest. Pinello's scoring panel appears automatically — no clicks needed.

2

Create your pin normally

Upload an image, write your title and description, pick a board, add topics. Pinello scores each dimension live as you type.

3

Fix what's weak, publish what's strong

Red/orange bars? Expand for specific tips. One-click AI rewrite generates optimized alternatives you can paste. Green? Ship it with confidence.

5 dimensions scored live

🖼️

Image fitness

Aspect ratio (2:3 ideal), resolution, brightness/contrast, text overlay detection — all analyzed locally in your browser.

📌

Board fit

Keyword relevance between your pin content and the selected board. Catches mismatched categorization before you publish.

✏️

Title SEO

Length, keyword placement, actionable phrasing, and AI coherence check. Plus gibberish detection to catch placeholder text.

📝

Description quality

Length optimization (350-500 chars), hashtag balance, CTA detection, readability scoring.

#️⃣

Hashtag coverage

Counts both inline #hashtags and tagged topics together. Flags too few, too many, or missing tags.

✨

AI rewrite (one click)

Weak title or description? Click "Copy AI rewrite" for 2 optimized alternatives. Paste what you like, discard the rest.

FAQ

Is Pinello free?

Yes. Free tier includes 50 AI scores/month and 5 AI rewrites/month. Structural scoring (image, hashtags, keyword overlap) is always unlimited. Pro tier with unlimited AI features is coming soon.

Does it work with Pinterest business accounts?

Yes. Pinello works on any Pinterest account type (personal, business, creator) — it detects the pin-creation UI regardless of account tier.

Does it modify my pins automatically?

Never. Pinello is "copy-only" — AI suggestions go to your clipboard. You paste what you want, where you want. No auto-injection into Pinterest fields.

What data does Pinello send to its servers?

Only the text (title/description) you choose to score via AI — processed ephemerally (not stored). Image analysis runs 100% locally. See our privacy policy for full details.

Is Pinello affiliated with Pinterest?

No. Pinello is an independent tool, not affiliated with or endorsed by Pinterest, Inc. Pinterest is a trademark of Pinterest, Inc.
