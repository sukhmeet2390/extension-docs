Frequently asked questions

How much does Privacy Blur for Discord cost?
You can install it free and start blurring right away. Pro is a $5 one-time purchase for lifetime access — no subscription, no recurring surprise.
Does it send Discord messages to a server?
No Discord message content, usernames, media, or member names are sent to Amrita Labs. Blur logic runs locally in your browser. Anonymous product analytics and billing events are covered in the privacy policy.
Who is this built for?
Gamers, streamers, content creators, moderators, coaches, and team collaborators use it to keep DMs, private servers, and internal channels hidden during live sharing.
Can I blur the channel list and member list before I start sharing?
Yes. You can independently blur messages, usernames, the channel and DM list, activity status, avatars, compose box, images and videos, and the member list before you go live.
Does it work in the Discord desktop app?
Privacy Blur works on Discord in Chrome at discord.com. The desktop app does not support Chrome extensions.
