Frequently asked questions

Is Privacy Blur for WhatsApp free?
Yes. Core blur zones and hover-to-reveal are free forever. Pro adds persistence, keyboard shortcuts, keyword rules, and sender rules for $5 one-time (lifetime).
Does it send my chats to a server?
No WhatsApp chat content, contact names, or media are sent to Amrita Labs. Blur logic runs locally in your browser. Anonymous product analytics and billing events are covered in the privacy policy.
Does it work in group chats and DMs?
Yes. It works on WhatsApp Web across direct messages, group chats, the chat sidebar, previews, and shared media.
Can I blur the chat list before I start sharing?
Yes. You can independently blur chat list names, preview text, profile photos, the active conversation, compose box, media, and status text.
Does it work in the WhatsApp desktop app?
Privacy Blur works on WhatsApp Web at web.whatsapp.com in Chrome. The desktop app does not support Chrome extensions.
