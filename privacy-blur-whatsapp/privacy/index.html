Privacy Policy — Privacy Blur for WhatsApp

Effective Date: 2026-07-21 · Last Updated: 2026-07-21

1. Who We Are

Privacy Blur for WhatsApp is operated by Amrita Labs, based in Canada.
Contact: hello@amrita-labs.com

2. What This Extension Does

Privacy Blur for WhatsApp blurs sensitive content (messages, contact names, chat previews, profile photos, media, compose text, and status details) on WhatsApp Web during screen sharing, video calls, and presentations. It operates primarily client-side within your browser on web.whatsapp.com.

4. What Leaves Your Browser

Very little. This extension performs its blur logic locally. External communication is limited to:

  • Payment processing: When you start a trial, upgrade, log in, or manage billing, ExtensionPay and Stripe handle payment-related flows. If you provide an email address during billing, it is handled by those providers.
  • Chrome Sync: Your extension preferences may sync across Chrome browsers via Google's built-in sync infrastructure.
  • Anonymous analytics: The extension sends non-content analytics events to PostHog, tagged with a random device ID, app version, and event metadata such as which blur zone was toggled.

We do not operate an app backend that receives your WhatsApp chats. No message content, media content, or phone-book-style contact data is sent to Amrita Labs.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Extension preferences and local identifiers: deleted when you uninstall the extension or clear extension data
  • Analytics events: retained by PostHog under its retention settings
  • Payment records: retained by ExtensionPay / Stripe per their retention policies
  • We retain no server-side copy of your WhatsApp messages or media

To delete local extension data: uninstall the extension and clear any remaining Chrome extension storage. For billing records, contact Stripe or ExtensionPay where applicable.

8. Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for users under 13 (COPPA) or under 16 in the EU (GDPR). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Security

  • Blur logic runs locally on WhatsApp Web in your browser
  • No custom Amrita Labs server receives WhatsApp chat content
  • Payment handled by Stripe via ExtensionPay
  • Anonymous analytics only — no chat text or media payloads
  • Extension access is limited to web.whatsapp.com plus ExtensionPay callback pages needed for billing flow
