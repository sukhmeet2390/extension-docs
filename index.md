## Chrome Extensions

### [EmailSubjectScore Pro](/gmail-subject-score/)
Score your Gmail subject lines for spam risk, clarity, and urgency — in real time, right inside compose. AI-powered rewrite suggestions.

→ [Install from Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/emailsubjectscore-pro-sub/dleffnbdjohkmmkbojehdpkgkmpofmgm) · [Privacy](/gmail-subject-score/privacy) · [Terms](/gmail-subject-score/terms)

### [Pinello — Live Pin Score & SEO](/pinello/)
Score Pinterest pins across 5 dimensions — image, board fit, title, description, and hashtags — as you create them. With one-click AI rewrite.

→ [Install from Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/pinello/kmelegdnljikegoemigfgnpcbllmkije) · [Privacy](/pinello/privacy) · [Terms](/pinello/terms)
